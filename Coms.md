## Transforming technical concepts into non-technical language

### Tailor the Depth to the Audience
-	Executives: Focus on business impact, timelines, and ROI.
- Managers: Add some operational details (resources, coordination)
- Technical teams: Include architecture, dependencies, and implementation details.

>	Tips: If the ask how something works, give a concise, high-level answers before diving into details.
